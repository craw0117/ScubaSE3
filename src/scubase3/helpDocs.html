1. Calculation Menu

1.1 MOD

Maximum Operating Depth is the depth of the dive that can be undertaken based on the partial pressure of gas. Inputs for the application are Partial Pressure and Oxygen Percentage.

1.1.1 Partial Pressure(Pg)

This can be added manually to two decimal places or selected through the use of the up and down buttons. The range for the Partial Pressure is between 1.1 to 1.6 bars.

1.1.2 Oxygen Percentage(Fg)

This can be added manually or selected through the use of the up and down buttons next to the percentage. the percentage is inputted as a decimal between 0.22 to 0.50.

1.1.3 MOD Results

Click on calculate for the results to be presented the maximum operating depths in metres.

1.2 Best Mix(Still in Development)

Best Mix calculates what ratio of oxygen is best for a particular depth. Inputs for the application are Partial Pressure and Depth.

1.2.1 Partial Pressure(Pg)

This can be added manually to two decimal places or selected through the use of the up and down buttons. The range for the Partial Pressure is between 1.1 to 1.6 bars.

1.2.2 Depth(m)

This can be added manualy or selected through the up and down buttons. the current range is between 0-100 metres.

1.2.3 Results

Click on calculate for the results to be presented for the Oxygen Percentage as a decimal

1.3 PP(Still in Development)

Partial Pressure calculates what the pressure of the oxygen should be based on the Oxygen percentage and the depth of the dive. Inputs for the application are Oxygen Percentage and Depth.

1.3.1 Oxygen Percentage(Fg)

This can be manually or selected through the use of the up and down buttons next to the percentage. The percentage is inputted as a decimal between 0.22 to 0.50.

1.3.2 Depth(m)

This can be added manually or selected through the up and down buttons. The current range is between 0-100 metres.

1.3.3 Results

Click on calculate for the results to be presented for the pressure in ata.

1.4 SMOD(Still in Development)

Maximum Operating Depth is the depth of the dive that can be undertaken based on the partial pressure of gas. Standard Maximum Operating Depth has the Partial Pressure fixed at 1.4Pg. Input for the application is Oxygen Percentage

1.4.1 Oxygen Percentage(Fg)

This can be added manually or selected through the use of the up and down buttons next to the percentage. The percentage is inputted as a decimal between 0.22 to 0.50

1.4.2 Results

Click on calculate for the results to be presented for the maximum operating depth in metres
